Guoinol is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and secure environment for all users. 
We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

This document outlines the child safety standards followed by the Guoinol application and its developer.

---

## 1. Zero Tolerance for CSAE

Guoinol strictly prohibits:
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Sexual content involving minors
- Grooming, exploitation, or endangerment of children
- Any attempt to solicit or distribute content involving minors

Any violation of this policy results in **immediate account termination** and may be reported to 
relevant law enforcement authorities in accordance with applicable laws.

---

## 2. Age Restrictions

Guoinol is intended **only for users aged 18 years and above**.

We do not knowingly allow minors to:
- Register accounts
- Use the app
- Access social or messaging features

Accounts suspected of belonging to minors may be suspended or removed after review.

---

## 3. In-App Reporting & User Feedback

Guoinol provides in-app mechanisms that allow users to:
- Report inappropriate content
- Report user profiles or messages
- Block other users
- Submit safety-related feedback

Reports related to child safety are reviewed with priority.

---

## 4. Handling of CSAM Reports

If Guoinol becomes aware of any content or activity related to child sexual abuse or exploitation:

- The content is immediately removed
- The associated account is permanently banned
- The incident may be reported to appropriate authorities as required by law

Guoinol cooperates with lawful requests from authorities investigating CSAE-related matters.

---

## 5. Legal Compliance

Guoinol complies with all applicable:
- Child protection laws
- Online safety regulations
- Platform policies related to child safety
